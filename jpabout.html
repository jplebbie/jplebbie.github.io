ABOUT THE PHOTOGRAPHER


Hello, I'm J.P Lebbie. I'm a lifestyle and portrait photographer.
I currently reside in Baltimore, Maryland and I'm in the Army.
I enjoy traveling, being outdoors, and music. I'm also a huge gear nerd.
Welcome to my website.

I aim to illustrate emotion and document culture through the moments I capture.
I'm extremely client-focused and determined to provide the best shots possible.
I bring a handy stool with me to every shoot which allows me to work with different angles,
giving me the ability to take a better shot that I otherwise wouldn't be able to acheive.
I would love to get the chance to work with you and portray your story.
Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio.
